Social Media Etiquettes

Social media platforms allow users to interact and share content. Here are some essential etiquettes:

A) Be Secure

Choose a strong password: Regularly update and secure your social media accounts with strong passwords.

Know who you befriend: Avoid accepting requests from unknown people to protect your safety.

B) Be Reliable

Think before uploading: Once uploaded, files stay on remote servers; be cautious about sharing sensitive content.
